Dare to be you

 I see your big heart. Don't hide it to fit into this shallow word, open up!

 Never afraid to be you. The real you, That's the best you and the only you anyone needs to know.

 Someone won't like it. They're not meant for you. Someone will love it, Those are you people. Anyone that doesn't love the real you, is not meant for you. Keep giving you all, to everyone. shine bright, like the sun.

 I see you, I see you don't fit it, I see you're different, but you will find your place, I see you're different and what a blessing that is!

 Don't settle for people who dim your light, wait for those who light you up. Wait for the spark, wait for the magic. The right people will show up in your life, if you're patient and true to youself.

 I see you, I see your big dreams.
 Keep chasing them, keep fighting for them. but never lose sight of what's really important.

 You have it all, and you deserve it all!
