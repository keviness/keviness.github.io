Surround Yourself with Friendship

 From today, tell yourself, I have everything I need within myself. The only approval I'll ever need is mine.
 I choose to stop apologizing for being me. I have the power to change my world.

 Now I must warn you that when you make a decision to be different, it won't all be rosy. You would lose some friends. Many will not go on this journey of acting out the normal with you. Some will reprimand you and drain you. But that's fine, Because not everyone is suppose to walk with you through this journey called life.

 In everything you do, It's important that you surround yourself with the rigth kind of friendship. Things are never quite as scary when you have real friends.
 And setting yourself apart attracts that kind of friendship.

 Those friends who make your problems their problems, just so you don't have to go through them alone.
